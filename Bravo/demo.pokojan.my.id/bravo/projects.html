ALLBARBER

About

Allbarber built a comedic empire by creating shareable video- and photo-based content that makes people laugh. Nearing their fifteenth anniversary, they were expanding into mobile and daily products for users and, with that, identified the need for a brand refresh and more complete brand system.

The result of their branding process was a realization of what the brand should be: something that honors the Allbarber personality while exploring a refined design style drawing inspiration from three historic styles (Art Nouveau, Modernism, and Contemporary). The new iteration includes but is not limited to a logo and wordmark refresh, primary and secondary typography, ascribed color palette, and exploration of other secondary brand elements that create a look that supports the brand throughout, and without the need for a dominating, omnipresent logo.
